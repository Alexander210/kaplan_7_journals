What are the five different Java data types? What are they used for?
1.Int - Integer, whole number, no decimal. Ex: 4,3,2,-1,-2

2.Float - 32bit

3.Short, long, double - 16, 64, 64 - These are for decimal modes

4.Char - Character "A" "I" ";"

5.String - A collection of chars "abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz"
